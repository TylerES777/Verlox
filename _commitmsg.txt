Freeze diagram toggle until prose is fully revealed; fade between views

The button was enabling at status=done even though reveal-smoothing
was still streaming characters into finalResponse - clicking in that
window asked the backend to diagram a half-empty prose, producing the
empty Nothing-to-Visualise-Yet callout. The toggle now stays disabled
until status is terminal AND finalResponse has caught up to
pendingResponse.

View switches now fade-in via the existing 200ms keyframe instead of
snapping. The animation is keyed on a tick that bumps per toggle so
the very first render does not fade in along with reveal-smoothing.
